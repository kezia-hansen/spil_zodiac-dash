A whimsical journey awaits

Zodiac Dash

Once upon a time, in a magical sky kingdom, there was a brave little fox named Seira who wanted his own special belly sign. To make his dream come true, Seira needs your help to catch as many sparkling Aries gems as possible. But watch out! Dangerous Skull gems are also hiding among the clouds, ready to ruin Seira’s quest. Clicking on even one of these bad gems will cost you a life. You need to catch 10 good gems to help Seira get his wish. But be careful not to collect more than three bad gems, or you’ll have to start again. Are you ready to join Seira and fly high in the sky to catch the gems?
